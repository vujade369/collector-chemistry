# Start Here

Constellate is a social mirror for what people keep.

It helps people see the pattern in what they collect, attend, support, and remember — then discover where that pattern overlaps with someone else’s.

## Current focus

The current product starts with public NFT wallets.

The long-term product can expand into:
- tickets
- memberships
- places
- events
- purchases
- communities
- games
- cultural artifacts

## Core thesis

What you keep becomes a pattern.  
What overlaps becomes recognition.
